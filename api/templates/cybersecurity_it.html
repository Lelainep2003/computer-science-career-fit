CYBERSECURITY AND IT
IT and cybersecurity are concerned with the protection of data. This involves identifying where the data exists and what it is, as well as creating ways to protect it. It is common for an organization to combine these two teams or roles into one, especially as data continues to move to online formats and away from physical formats.
In practice, the roles between IT and cybersecurity professionals will overlap. Both approaches have the end goal of protecting sensitive company data- through a combination of technologies and physical processes.
